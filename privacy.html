Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At Visaura, your privacy and data security are our top priorities. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your personal information and documents.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information necessary to provide our services, which includes your account details (email, name) and the temporary upload of your visa application documents.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your documents are strictly used for the purpose of compiling and formatting your visa application packet. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal documents with any third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

All document processing is performed securely. Once your final visa packet is generated and downloaded, uploaded files are systematically purged from our active processing servers. Account data is secured using industry-standard encryption via Firebase Auth.

4. Payment Information

We process payments securely through Stripe. We never store your full credit card details on our servers.
